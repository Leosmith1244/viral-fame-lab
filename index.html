About the Workshop

Learn to create content that captivates, converts, and goes viral

October 16th 2025 • 7PM to 8:30PM Central Time • Online via Zoom

In today's digital landscape, creating content that stands out is more challenging than ever. The Viral Fame Lab workshop is designed specifically for Toastmasters members who want to amplify their club's online presence and attract new members through powerful, engaging content.

Drawing on time-tested principles from Aristotle's Rhetorical Triangle and modern digital marketing strategies, this workshop will equip you with practical techniques to create content that resonates, engages, and spreads.

What You'll Learn

  • The psychology behind viral content
  • How to hook viewers in just 3 seconds
  • Ethical clickbait techniques that work
  • How to leverage trending topics for maximum reach
  • Practical applications for Toastmasters promotion
