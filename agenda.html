PR Pros Toastmasters Club Meeting Agenda

Date: October 16, 2025

Time: 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM Central Time

Location: Virtual Meeting (Zoom link: [Insert Link])

Objective: Learn powerful social media strategies to boost Toastmasters visibility and engagement, followed by a Q&A and club business discussion.

Duration: 90 minutes

Meeting Agenda

1
7:00 PM - 7:10 PM

Welcome & Introductions

What to Expect:

  • Warm welcome from our Club President or host
  • Quick icebreaker: Share your name and favorite social media platform in the chat
  • Overview of today's exciting Social Media Success Workshop!

Your Role: Introduce yourself in the chat and get ready to learn!

2
7:10 PM - 7:59 PM

Social Media Success Workshop

Join us for four short talks with hands-on activities to make your Toastmasters posts go viral!

7:10 PM - 7:20 PM: The 3-Second Rule

  • Speaker: Club Member
  • What You'll Learn: How to hook audiences in 3 seconds with bold visuals, punchy intros, and clear value
  • Activity: Live TikTok Critique – Analyze real posts in breakout rooms to spot what hooks or flops

7:23 PM - 7:33 PM: Dopamine Hacks

  • Speaker: Club Member
  • What You'll Learn: Trigger shares with emotions like outrage, awe, or FOMO
  • Activity: Rewrite a Dull Post – Transform a boring club post into a shareable hit in breakout rooms

7:36 PM - 7:46 PM: The Dark Arts of Clickbait

  • Speaker: Club Member
  • What You'll Learn: Craft ethical, irresistible headlines using listicles, questions, or bold claims
  • Activity: Create a Scandalous-But-True Headline – Write a catchy headline in breakout rooms and vote for the best!

7:49 PM - 7:59 PM: Trend-Jacking Olympics

  • Speaker: Club Member
  • What You'll Learn: Leverage trending memes or topics to promote Toastmasters
  • Activity: Meme Rebrand Race – Rebrand a trend for a club event in breakout rooms
3
7:59 PM - 8:04 PM

Q&A Session

What to Expect: Ask questions about the strategies or share thoughts via Zoom's hand-raise or chat.

Your Role: Jump in with a question or idea to deepen your learning!

4
8:04 PM - 8:14 PM

Attendee Comments

What to Expect: Share one strategy you're excited to try and how you'll use it for our club.

Your Role: Use the hand-raise feature or chat to contribute your thoughts!

5
8:14 PM - 8:24 PM

PR Pros Club Business Meeting

What to Expect:

  • Updates on upcoming events, memberships, and action items
  • Chance to volunteer (e.g., create a viral post!)

Your Role: Listen and offer ideas for club growth.

6
8:24 PM - 8:30 PM

Wrap-Up

What to Expect:

  • Final thanks and a challenge: Create one social media post this week using a workshop strategy
  • Digital handouts shared in chat or email
  • Quick feedback survey (Google Form link in chat)

Your Role: Share your post in our club chat and complete the survey to help us improve!
